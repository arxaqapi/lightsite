How to copy the inside of a folder in linux:

  • Using rsync: (fav)
    rsync -r source/ destination
    do not forget the "/" after source to not copy the folder containg the files.
  • Using cp:
    cp -a /source/. /dest/
    The `.` allows to copy all files and folders including hidden ones.
